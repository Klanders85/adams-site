About Us

Company History

Metro Air & Heat has been family owned and operated company for over 35 years. Proudly serving Tarrant County. Unlike most "national" corporations, you will be talking directly to the sole owners of the company, and deal with us each and every time. That is one of the many reason why service is more valuable to us than profit.The company was founded by Aaron Weeks 1978. Once he handed over the reigns to his son Tim, the company flourished. Fast foward to today where we have Adam Olsen (Tim’s nephew), who has been a huge part of the company for over 7 years now and is about to take the reigns from his uncle and grow the company even further.
